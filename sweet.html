Overview
Unified crypto asset management platform design

We are designing a unified crypto asset management platform that serves as an essential bridge, integrating purchases and data from diverse external crypto platforms. Our core objective is to deliver a seamless, human-centered UX/UI experience, enabling users to effortlessly consolidate purchase reviews, manage payment methods, and maintain account information across all devices via responsive interfaces.

PROBLEM

Problem statement

Sweet NFT is a comprehensive digital collectibles ecosystem where users collect and trade NFTs across diverse sports and topics via a Main Platform governing specialized Sub-Platforms. Due to compliance and specialization needs, each Sub-Platform currently requires separate account registration, dedicated digital wallets, and localized payment processing, creating a significant usability challenge as users must manage multiple distinct accounts within the same ecosystem

How might we centralize the management of user accounts, payment methods, and activity logs across multiple, disparate trading sites to reduce complexity and eliminate the need for redundant configuration?

our-users
Key user insights

We conducted thorough competitor benchmarking and qualitative user interviews to identify the most valued features and experience touch-points. This foundational research yielded essential deep thoughts and uncovered crucial user pain points, leading to the main insights detailed below.

Fasten purchase process
Implementing a secondary payment method system to ensure transaction reliability and mitigate the risk of failed user purchases.
Universal payment setting
Providing universal payment setting synchronization to streamline management and ensure consistency for purchases across all connected platforms
Enabling history checking
A core customer expectation is the ability to view unified activity and purchase logs derived from all integrated platforms.
Tiered account security
Implementing a tiered verification structure that ensures robust security and protection tailored to different levels of user account access and sensitive operations.

FINAL DESIGN
Design library

We established a comprehensive Design System to ensure visual consistency and strict adherence to company branding across all products through unified styling. The utilization of elements and components from this system was instrumental in significantly increasing the efficiency and productivity of the design workflow.

Desktop key screens
Using balance to purchase

To mitigate potential transaction failures caused by unsuccessful payment processing, a Platform Balance feature was developed. By enabling users to pre-load funds, this feature ensures high purchase reliability and streamlines the transaction flow, directly leading to a faster and more successful product acquisition

Managing payment method

We designed a centralized feature for seamless payment method management, allowing users to add, edit, or remove existing cards and bank accounts. This single configuration point ensures that updated payment information is immediately synchronized and utilized across all Sweet-related sub-platforms.

Checking activity history across platforms

We designed the Unified Activity Log to offer full transparency into user actions. This page tracks key events such as purchases, trades, fund additions, and gifts across the entire platform ecosystem, allowing users to drill down into the transaction history for specific item details and review

Providing a secured environment

We utilize Auth0 to secure the password reset process, and further strengthen account protection by implementing essential security layers, including Identity Verification and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

Takeaways

Project Outcomes and Achievements

To ensure an efficient and user-centric design outcome, our process focuses on three foundational principles:
  • Empathy-Driven Information Architecture: We adopt the user's perspective to rigorously determine how to present and structure information most efficiently.
  • Proactive Responsiveness: Different screen sizes and device constraints are considered during the initial ideation phase to guarantee an optimized experience across all platforms.
  • Problem Clarity for Efficiency: Thoroughly clarifying user needs and the core problem statement early in the process prevents unnecessary scope creep and costly design revisions later in the development cycle
